About me

Hello, I am Allie Valder. I am 24 and new to coding. I graduated from the Univeristy of North Carolina at Charlotte in May of 2017. I have a dual Batchelors of Fine Arts in Ceramics and Art Education. I taught middle school art for a year and decided that teaching was simultaneously too draining and not challenging enough for me. I have always wanted to learn coding and love the problem solving and creative aspects that it offers.

I have two cats, Xhena and Anders, whom are both rescues. Xhena is a 4 year old tortoise shell. she's the most loving kitty and will be your bestfriend if you have treats. Anders is a 1 year old tabby. He is loving and spunky but thinks he owns the place. He is named after the character Anders from the Dragon Age videogame series.

In my free time I enjoy playing videogames, working out, hiking, camping, watching movies and TV, and traveling when I can. I am an artist who specializes in ceramic sculptures and watercolor paintings. I am part of a D&D group where I play a half elf rougue named Iris.
